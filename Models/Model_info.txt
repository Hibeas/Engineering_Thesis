Face Landmarker
    Pretrained Model by Google Mediapipe. It allows us to get:
        - 478 Landmarks - which are points of x, y and z coordnates from your face
        - 52 Face Blandshapes - based on the movment of those points, the model scores from 0.0 to 1.0 of 52 diffrent facial expressions. 
          These blendshapes conform to the FACS (Facial Action Coding System) standard, which is widely used in 3D character animation (like Metahumans in Unreal Engine) and sentiment analysis
    
    More info and download: https://ai.google.dev/edge/mediapipe/solutions/vision/face_landmarker
